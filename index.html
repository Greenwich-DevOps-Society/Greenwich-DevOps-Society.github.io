Greenwich DevOps Society

We are a student-led society focused on practical projects, collaborative challenges, and regular events that help members build technical skills and career confidence.

What We Do Join Us

Focus Area

Women in Tech

We run a weekly Women in Tech session led by a woman on the course. It is a dedicated space for networking, peer support, confidence building, and practical skill development.

The session is open to students who want a more supportive environment to learn, ask questions, and grow with others.

Group Challenges

Work through coding and DevOps tasks together to improve practical problem-solving and teamwork.

Student-Led Projects

Build projects as a group and gain experience you can use in your portfolio, CV, and interviews.

Events and Meetups

Attend computing events, skill sessions, and informal meetups with other students in the community.

Join Greenwich DevOps Society

New members are welcome from all technical backgrounds. We also run a weekly women-in-tech meeting led by a woman on the course to support mentoring, connection, and an inclusive environment.
